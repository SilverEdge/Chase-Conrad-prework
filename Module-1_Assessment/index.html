About me

Hello! My name is Chase Conrad. I like to play soccer. My favorite color is orange, although I will admit that teal is the real deal! Since I was young, I've enjoyed putting together websites for my friends and family. I have a degree in Economics from UW Madison. Professionally, I have worked as a janitor, clerk, and data scientist. My favorite play is "King Lear" by William Shakespeare. My dream is to code for a living!

I have three brothers and enjoy playing ping pong, soccer, hacky sack and basketball with them. I like to study financial markets, usually evaluating current conditions through the lense of options strategies. One of my favorite strategies is to place a vertical long put on VXX after a spike in volatility.

If you are still reading this, then you must be pretty bored, so I will give you some more facts about me. I was born in Longmont, Colorado to LeAnn and Mickey Conrad. At age 5, my family moved to Wisconsin where I have resided to this day. As a child my favorite hobby was building Lego sets.
